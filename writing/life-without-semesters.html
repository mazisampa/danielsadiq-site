Life Without Semesters

We do not need a new season to begin.

July 21, 2026 · 2 min read

School gives us semesters. Clear beginnings. Clear endings. A date when the work starts and a date when it is judged.

Then school ends, but many of us keep waiting for the next semester. We wait for January. We wait for Monday. We wait for confidence. We wait for permission.

Real life does not organize itself so neatly. There is no registration period for becoming who you are supposed to become.

You begin before you feel ready. You learn while moving. You become by doing.

The calendar is useful. But it is not in charge. Today is enough of a beginning.
