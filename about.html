About Daniel

I help people and organizations prepare for what comes next.

Transformation is rarely a technology problem alone. It is a problem of imagination, behavior, systems and trust.

My career has moved across engineering, cloud technology, DevOps, product management, organizational transformation, teaching and leadership.

Today, I work with leaders and organizations exploring how artificial intelligence can create real value, how technology teams can operate more effectively, and how people can continue learning in a world that refuses to stand still.

I am an AI Transformation Coach, technology leader and teacher. I have trained hundreds of professionals and spent years helping teams translate ambitious strategies into practical ways of working.

I also write about leadership, creativity, work, transformation and the idea I call Life Without Semesters: the belief that we do not have to wait for permission, a new year or a perfect moment to begin becoming who we are capable of becoming.
